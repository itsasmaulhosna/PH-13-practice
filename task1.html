Welcome to my travel blog where i share my travel experiences.

Last december i visited Cox's Bazar with my family. It was an ammazing experience. The beach was beautiful and the weather was perfect for a vacation.
We enjoyed swimming in the sea and building sandcastles on the shore. We also tried some delicious seafood at a local resturant.
Overall, it was a memorable trip and I can't wait to visit again soon

Special places of Cox's Bazar

  1. Laboni beach
    • The most popular beach in Cox's Bazar
    • Best for sunset views and evening walks
  2. Inani Beach
    • Known for coral stons and clear water
    • Popular for snorkeling and diving
  3. Himchori National Park
    • Known for its lush greenery and biodiversity
    • Great for hiking and bird watching
  4. Marine Drive
    • A beautiful coastal road with scenic views
    • Perfect for evening walks and photography
  5. Burmess Market
    • A bustling local market with traditional goods
  6. Moheshkhali Island
    • Famouse for its unique geography and curious wildlife

There are many more places to explore in Cox's Bazar,each offering unique experience.
